A circle is a SHAPE consisting of all points in a plane that are at a given distance from a given point, the centre; equivalently it is the curve traced out by a point that moves in a plane so that its distance from a given point is constant. ~Wikipedia

The circle is a UNIVERSAL SYMBOL that represents the ideas of totality, wholeness, original perfection, the Self, the infinite, eternity, timelessness, and cyclic movement. It symbolizes God ('God is a circle whose centre is everywhere and whose circumference is nowhere' (Hermes Trismegistus)).

Circles MAKE LIFE POSSIBLE. Circles are intricate parts of nature. The sun and moon are circles. Time occurs in repetitive cycles in the form of days, months, years and seasons. ~Nina Jay
